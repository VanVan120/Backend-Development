HIPAA Compliance

Our commitment to protecting Protected Health Information (PHI).

Overview

Oral AI is fully committed to compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) of 1996. We have implemented robust physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of all Protected Health Information (PHI) processed by our system.

Technical Safeguards
  • • End-to-end encryption (AES-256) for data transmission.
  • • Automatic session timeouts and secure authentication.
  • • Strict access controls and audit logs for all data access.
Administrative Safeguards
  • • Regular security training for all personnel.
  • • Business Associate Agreements (BAA) with all vendors.
  • • Comprehensive incident response and breach notification plans.

Data Handling & De-identification

Our AI models are trained on de-identified datasets in strict accordance with the HIPAA Privacy Rule. When you upload patient imagery for analysis:

  1. Data is processed in a transient, secure memory state.
  2. No PHI is permanently stored on our inference servers unless explicitly authorized for patient records.
  3. All metadata is stripped before any potential use in model improvement (if opted-in).
Business Associate Agreement (BAA)

For enterprise clients and healthcare institutions, we offer a signed Business Associate Agreement (BAA) to formalize our responsibilities regarding your PHI. Please contact our compliance team to request a BAA.
