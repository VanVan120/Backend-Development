Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 4, 2025

1. Introduction

At Oral AI ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and the confidentiality of your medical data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered oral disease detection platform.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Health Information (PHI): Images of oral cavities, histopathology slides, and related diagnostic data uploaded for analysis.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, including access times and browser types.
  • Account Information: If you register, we collect your name, email address, and professional credentials (for clinicians).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • • To provide AI-driven diagnostic analysis and reports.
  • • To improve the accuracy of our machine learning models (using de-identified data only).
  • • To maintain the security and integrity of our platform.
  • • To comply with legal obligations and healthcare regulations.

4. Data Security & AI Processing

We employ state-of-the-art encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and in transit. Images uploaded for analysis are processed in secure, isolated environments. Unless explicitly consented to for research purposes, uploaded medical imagery is automatically deleted from our inference servers after the analysis session is complete.
